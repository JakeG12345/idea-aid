About

Idea AId is a webapp that aims to help people come up with ideas for, well, anything. Through the use of OpenAI's API, the app communicates with the GPT-4 Turbo chat bot in order to obtain data to create questions and then generate ideas from those questions.

Simply log in or sign up, click here or on the 'generator' link and answer the questions. Before you know it, an abundance of innovative, creative and practical ideas will be at your fingertips - ready for personal or commerical use. Enjoy!

This website was created by Jake Glick, Asher Waine and Jacob Turtledove in december of 2023 for the harvard CS50 course final project.
