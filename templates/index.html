About

Idea AId is a webapp that aims to help people come up with ideas for anything. Through the use of OpenAI's API, the app communicates with the GPT-4 Turbo chat bot in order to get data to make questions and then generate ideas from those questions.
The website was created by First Last, First Last and First Last in december of 2023 for the harvard CS50 course final project.
